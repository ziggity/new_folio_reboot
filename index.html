About me

Before entering the software industry, I honed my communication and collaborative problem-solving skills while working as a tech recruiter / talent sourcer. I practiced creatively finding talented developers, and breaking complex recruiting projects into manageable steps, and conveying market trends to hiring managers / account managers.

I am currently seeking a position which will enable me to combine my tech skills with my problem solving skills. Specifically, my goal is to join a rapidly-growing company that values innovation and teamwork, that will enable me to continue learning, and that exerts a significant positive impact on its users’ daily lives.

Tech skills: Ruby on Rails, Javascript, Node, Express, React, Redux, Git, Github, HTML5, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap, ES6, CSS3, SCSS, Agile Development, Firebase, Heroku, MySQL, jQuery, Socket.io, Python, Java, D3, TypeScript, Angular
